Keeping Your Fleet on the Road, One Repair at a Time

About Haul Pulse

Haul Pulse was founded with a single mission—keeping America’s trucks and fleets running strong. As experienced diesel technicians and passionate problem-solvers, we know how critical it is to get your rig or fleet vehicle back on the road quickly and safely. Whether you're an independent owner-operator or manage a commercial fleet, we treat every vehicle like it’s our own.

With decades of hands-on experience in diesel repair, Haul Pulse has built a reputation for quality workmanship, fair pricing, and fast turnaround times. We specialize in heavy-duty truck diagnostics, DOT inspections, engine and transmission repair, brake systems, and comprehensive fleet maintenance. Our shop is equipped with advanced diagnostic tools and staffed by certified diesel mechanics who know how to fix complex issues right the first time.

We proudly serve long-haul drivers, regional carriers, and local trucking companies across the area. What sets us apart is our commitment to customer satisfaction. We communicate clearly, never overpromise, and always deliver top-tier results. From preventive maintenance to emergency repairs, Haul Pulse is your reliable partner in minimizing downtime and maximizing safety.

At Haul Pulse, we understand that time is money in the trucking business. That’s why we offer flexible scheduling, efficient service, and the kind of expertise that only comes from years of doing the job right. Our team stays up-to-date on the latest diesel technologies and industry best practices to keep your fleet compliant, reliable, and road-ready.

We’re more than a repair shop—we’re part of the backbone of the trucking community. Whether you're rolling through town or based nearby, our goal is to earn your trust through quality service and honest support. Choose Haul Pulse for diesel repair you can count on, every mile of the way.
